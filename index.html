Why a Pet-Friendly City?

Pet-friendly cities are happier, healthier, and more connected. They promote well-being, social engagement, and respect for animals.

Our Initiatives

Pet Parks

Open spaces for dogs to run and play safely.

Transit Access

Allowing pets in city metros, buses, and rickshaws.

Pet-Friendly Cafes

Outdoor and indoor spaces where pets are welcome.
